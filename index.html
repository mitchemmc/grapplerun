About

Grapple Run is a multiplayer couch platformer-racer where you race your friends to the top of a vertically scrolling map using your trusty grappling hook to swing yourself to victory. Utilize random power-ups and the environment to hinder others and boost yourself to the treasure above!

A student project, Grapple Run was created over a 10 week period in early 2017 by Mitch McCaffrey (programming, design), Ben Ritthichai (programming), Kevin Vo (programming) and Ben Bartlett (audio).

To play use the appropriate download link above for your platform. Currently the game supports 1-4 XBox 360, XBox One and PS3 controllers with PS4 controller support experimental on both platforms. No mouse and keyboard is supported at this time.
